# Privacy Policy

This application respects your privacy:

- No personal data is collected, stored, or shared with third parties.
- The tool processes videos locally on your machine, unless you configure external APIs (e.g., TikTok).
- Any access tokens or API keys you provide are used only to authenticate requests you explicitly make.
- We do not sell or share any information with third parties.

If you have any questions about this policy, you may stop using the application at any time.
